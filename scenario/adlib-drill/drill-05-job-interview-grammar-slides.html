模範解答

まずはこの3文を覚える

背景 → 担当・行動 → 結果の順（STAR法）

解説 · ①

In my previous role — 前職では

背景を1文で置く（STAR の S）

解説 · ②

I was responsible for — 担当していた

困難と自分の行動を入れる

解説 · ③

As a result — その結果

数字で成果を締める

言い換え

トーンを調整する

背景 → 行動 → 結果の骨格は同じ

フルIn my previous role, I was responsible for a major product launch. We faced a tight deadline, but I coordinated the team and we delivered on time. As a result, sales exceeded our target by fifteen percent.
短くAt my last company, I led a project under a tight deadline. We delivered on time, so sales beat our target.
行動強調When I was responsible for a launch, the deadline was tight. I coordinated the team, and we delivered on time.
数字なしIn my previous role, I handled a difficult project. I worked with the team and we finished ahead of schedule.
