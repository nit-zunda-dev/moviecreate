模範解答

まずはこの2文を覚える

ビジョン → テーマ＋「なぜ今ここで」

解説 · ①

Imagine a world where — 理想の世界を描く

聴衆の頭に映像を浮かべさせるフック

解説 · ②

Today I want to talk about — テーマ宣言

なぜ重要かを聴衆に結びつける

解説 · ③

But first, let me ask you — 聴衆への問い

模範2文だけでもOK。余裕があれば足す

言い換え

尺と緊張度で調整

ビジョン → テーマの骨格は同じ

フル（模範）Imagine a world where every employee leaves work on time, feeling energized. Today I want to talk about Japan's work-style reform—and why it matters not just for Japan, but for all of us in this room.
＋問いかけ... But first, let me ask you: when was the last time you left the office feeling truly energized?
短くImagine everyone going home on time. Today, Japan's work-style reform—and why it matters to you.
最低限Imagine better work-life balance. Today I want to talk about Japan's work-style reform. That's it for the opening.
